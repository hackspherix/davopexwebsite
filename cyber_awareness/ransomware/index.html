Understanding Ransomware Attacks

2026 Edition 5 Minutes Beginner

Ransomware has evolved from simple lockers to double-exfiltration extortion. In this module, we trace the infection path from initial delivery to the final encryption event. We analyze how attackers purge Volume Shadow Copies (VSS), achieve persistence via Registry keys, and exfiltrate sensitive data before triggering the payload to maximize leverage against the victim.

Key Learning Points

  • Infection Vector Analysis
  • VSS & Shadow Copy Purging
  • Registry Persistence Keys
  • Double-Extortion Logic
  • Post-Invention Recovery Tips
